% XP Table & Promotion Checklists
% Canonical award table, caps, verification, and promotion rubrics

1) Canonical XP award table (recommended starting values — tune later)
- Foundational course completion (Rookie bootcamp): +50 XP (one-time)
- Intermediate course / discipline primer: +150 XP
- Verified small task (code, doc, campaign asset): +10–100 XP (tiered by complexity)
  - Small task (simple): +10–25 XP
  - Moderate task: +50 XP
  - Complex task: +75–100 XP
- Project milestone (client acceptance): +200–500 XP depending on milestone value
- Lead project to completion (verified): +1,000 XP
- Publish reusable asset / major resource: +300 XP
- Mentor a Rookie for 3 months (verified outcomes): +200 XP
- Critical bug / urgent fix (production): +250 XP

2) Caps & decay
- Daily cap (task awards): 500 XP/day
- Monthly cap (routine tasks): 2,500 XP/month (exemptions require Governance review)
- Inactivity penalty: 7 consecutive days with no logged contribution → −100 XP
- Long inactivity optional decay: after 90 consecutive inactive days → −5% XP (recommendation)
- Fraud revocation: Governance may revoke awards after investigation; revocations documented with reasons and appeal path.

3) Verification ladder
- Auto (≤99 XP awards typically): system-verified completions (course certificate, CI passing, issue closed with linked artifact).
- Peer (100–999 XP awards): 1 independent peer confirmation required.
- Senior (≥1,000 XP awards): require manager/discipline reviewer + one peer OR two peer endorsements; cross-discipline impact requires a discipline reviewer from affected tracks.

4) Promotion checklists
- General rule: XP threshold + required evidence + time-in-rank minimum (where applicable).

Adept (Rank 2) — Eligibility: 100 XP
- Evidence: completion of foundational course + 2 verified small tasks.
- No time-in-rank minimum.

Veteran (Rank 3) — Eligibility: 1,000 XP
- Evidence:
  - At least 3 verified medium tasks or 1 project milestone + 1 medium task.
  - Peer references (2 recommended).
  - Demonstrated competence in role (mentor sign-off or lead evidence).
- Time-in-rank: min 3 months active as Adept recommended.

Commander (Rank 4) — Eligibility: 10,000 XP
- Evidence:
  - Lead or co-led at least one cross-discipline project with measurable outcomes.
  - Documented project artifacts, stakeholder feedback, or client acceptance.
  - Discipline-specific checklist items satisfied (technical code quality, legal accuracy, campaign KPIs).
- Time-in-rank: min 6 months active as Veteran.
- Review: Panel includes at least one Overlord or senior reviewer.

Overlord (Rank 5) — Eligibility: 100,000 XP
- Evidence:
  - Multiple Commander-level projects with organizational impact.
  - Demonstrated leadership in governance or sustained stewardship.
- Time-in-rank: min 12 months as Commander.
- Review: Governance panel with Archon or elected representatives.

Archon (Rank 6) — Eligibility: 1,000,000+ XP
- Evidence:
  - Major, demonstrable system-level leadership, cross-organizational impact, and governance contributions.
- Review: Full Governance Board review; external advisor input recommended.

5) Promotion workflow (summary)
1. System flags eligibility or nomination submitted.
2. Candidate collects evidence package (templates available).
3. Panel review within SLA (14 days standard).
4. Decision recorded, rank updated, audit log entry created.
5. Appeals window: 30 days; appeal reviewed by independent panel within ~21 days.
