% Domain & Branding Checklist
% Quick steps to choose and protect the community name, plus logo prompts

1. Name selection
- Primary: Triad Protocol (if you prefer another, pick up to 3 finalists).

2. Domain checks (quick)
- Check triadprotocol.com, triadprotocol.org, triadprotocol.xyz
- If taken, try hyphen or shorter: triad-protocol.com, triad.io, triadhub.org

3. Trademark basics
- Quick search via Google and a trademark database in your country before public launch.
- If you plan to monetize or scale, consult IP counsel.

4. Logo prompts (3 concepts you can give a designer)
A) Shield & Triad
- Visual: three interlocking shapes (triangle) inside a subtle shield outline.
- Tone: privacy, trust, tri-discipline focus.
- Colors: deep teal + charcoal.

B) Nexus Circuit
- Visual: three nodes connected by a circular ring, minimalist tech style.
- Tone: collaboration, engineering, modern.
- Colors: electric blue + white on dark background.

C) Foundry Mark
- Visual: small anvil or stylized hammer with three dots representing disciplines.
- Tone: creation, craft, founders.
- Colors: warm amber + slate gray.

5. Small brand checklist
- One-sentence mission, tagline, and short paragraph for "About".
- Prepare a logo, favicon, and a single master color palette.
