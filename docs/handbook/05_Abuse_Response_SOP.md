% Abuse Response SOP
% Reporting, triage, investigation, protections, timelines, and templates

Purpose
-------
Provide an operational Standard Operating Procedure for handling reports of abuse, harassment, coercion, doxxing, fraud, or other violations.

Goals
- Rapid protection for victims.
- Fair, documented investigation.
- Proportionate and transparent outcomes.
- Minimize anonymity abuse while preserving privacy rights.

Reporting intake (how members report)
- Safe Channel: encrypted incident form (required fields: reporter handle, accused handle(s), summary, evidence links, urgency level).
- Anonymous reports allowed; collect contact method for follow-up (optional).

Triage (within 48 hours)
- Neutral Triage Officer reviews intake, assesses immediate risk.
- If immediate harm suspected: freeze suspect privileges, secure evidence, notify Governance.
- If low severity: assign investigator and target timeline.

Investigation (within 14 days)
- Investigator(s): independent panel (not conflicted), typically 1 investigator + 1 reviewer.
- Steps:
  - Gather evidence (audit logs, chat exports, campaign artifacts).
  - Interview (pseudonymous) parties and gather statements.
  - Preserve all data and ensure chain-of-custody.

Interim protections
- Temporary measures (short-term, documented):
  - Mute posting privileges in channel.
  - Remove from active client engagement (if safety risk).
  - Suspend awarding privileges (if XP manipulation suspected).
- Duration: time-limited (e.g., up to 14 days) unless extended by Governance.

Decision & sanctions
- Outcomes: no action, warning, probation, demotion, temporary or permanent removal, referral to legal authorities.
- Sanctions documented with rationale, evidence summary, and appeal instructions.

Appeals
- Appeal filed within 30 days.
- Independent panel (not involved in original decision) reviews and decides within 21 days.

Protection for reporters
- Maintain reporter anonymity if requested.
- Anti-retaliation: any retaliation is a separate offense with strict penalties.
