% Beginner Launch & Micro-project
% Concrete plan for 4–6 week starter cohort and first micro-project

Goal
- Get a small beginner cohort to practice documentation, verification, and teamwork. Deliver a small, verifiable outcome to build confidence.

Cohort size
- 5–12 participants (mix Tech, Legal, Media). Recruit 1–2 guides (experienced contributors or founders).

Week-by-week plan (4 weeks minimal)
Week 0: Prep
- Create Bootcamp content (privacy + ethics + discipline primer).
- Prepare templates (evidence, campaign brief, simple acceptance criteria).

Week 1: Onboarding (1–3 hours)
- Handles selected; Member Rules accepted.
- Bootcamp completed.
- Mentor pairing done.

Week 2–3: Micro-project execution
- Project examples (pick one):
  A) Landing page + outreach plan: Tech builds a landing page; Media creates 3 social posts and schedule; Legal drafts a short TOS/consent popover.
  B) Standard NDA + short explainer & outreach: Legal drafts; Tech builds downloadable; Media prepares a short campaign to recruit initial contributors.
- Milestones:
  - M1 (end week 2): draft deliverable + evidence uploaded.
  - M2 (end week 3): completed deliverable + acceptance notes.

Week 4: Reflection & documentation
- Present deliverable pseudonymously.
- Mentors sign off; small XP awards given.
- Collect feedback and adjust checklists.

Roles & expectations
- Micro-team size: 2–4 contributors.
- Time commitment: 3–5 hours/week.
- Deliverable criteria: linkable artifact + 100-word acceptance summary + reviewer sign-off.

Success metrics (beginner)
- 80% Bootcamp completion.
- At least one milestone completed by most members.
- No severe incidents.
- Positive participant feedback.
