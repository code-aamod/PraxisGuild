% Governance & Conflict of Interest Policy
% Board composition, selection, recusal, removal, and succession

Board composition (starter)
- Small community: 3-7 governance seats:
  - Up to 2 Founder ex-officio seats (Leader / Co‑Leader).
  - 1–2 elected member representatives (rotating annually).
  - 1 rotating Overlord/Commander delegate.
  - 1 external advisor (optional) for objective perspective.

Selection & rotation
- Elected seats: nominated and elected by active members (Rank ≥ Veteran), 1-year term.
- Rotating delegates: appointed for 6 months.
- External advisor: invite with defined advisory remit.

Conflict of interest & recusal
- Any board member with direct interest in a case must recuse (no voting) and declare the conflict.
- Recusals recorded in the audit log.

Removal & succession
- Removal of a Founder or board member: exceptional; requires 2/3 Board vote + documentation of cause and appeals window.
- Succession: resigning Founders may nominate successor; Board approval required (simple majority or special threshold as defined).

Transparency
- Post anonymized summaries of major decisions with rationale.
- Publish periodic governance reports (anonymized KPIs, changes to policy).

Ad hoc committees
- For sensitive investigations, form an independent review committee drawn from non-conflicted members and external advisors when feasible.
