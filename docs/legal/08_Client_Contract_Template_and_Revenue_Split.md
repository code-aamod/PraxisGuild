% Client Contract Template & Revenue Split Examples
% Plain-language starter contract and sample revenue splits for small engagements

Plain-language client contract (starter)
- Parties: "Client" and "Triad Protocol (via Contributor Team)".
- Scope: clear deliverables, milestones, acceptance criteria.
- Timeline: milestone dates.
- Payment: total fee, escrow mechanism, milestone release conditions.
- IP: default - IP assigned to Client on full payment; alternative: license model if contributors opt-in.
- Confidentiality: contributors and platform agree to confidentiality terms.
- Dispute resolution: try mediation first, escalate to arbitration / local court (choose jurisdiction).
- Liability: limit platform liability (cap on damages) and state that contributors are independent contractors.
- Governing law: specify jurisdiction in formal TOS.

Sample Revenue split (illustrative)
- Client payment: $1,000
- Platform fee: 15% → $150
- Remaining: $850 distributed among contributors
  - Lead (manager): 40% → $340
  - Contributors pool (split by contribution weight): 60% → $510 (distribute by agreed shares or XP-based formula)
- Payout cadence: post-acceptance within 14 days (or per milestone).

Alternative paid-sprint model
- Flat day-rate per contributor approved in advance.
- Platform holds commission on top for coordination.

Notes
- Always use escrow for first-time clients.
- Record all agreements as evidence in project artifacts.
- If contributors intend to retain reuse rights, capture license terms explicitly and reward contributors for reusable assets.

End of file.
